Cambodia

The Kingdom of Wonder.

Cambodia is known by most for its rugged and vast Angkor era ancient ruins, but it is also home to welcoming people, off-the-beaten-track locations, and a stunning southern coastline often overlooked by travelers.

Additionally, Cambodia boasts some of the regionâ€™s finest colonial hotels. This incredible variety means Cambodia is home to a wealth of exceptional experiences which are completely unique and remains an authentic adventure.

People & food

Enjoy Siam Reap and explore the rural life of Cambodia. Visit Tonle Sap, join a Khmer cooking class and experience a blessing ceremony. Discover Battambang by bicycle and try the delicious Street Food in Phnom Penh.

Exploration

Experience a homestay in Kampong Thom, explore the small island of Koh Trong by bike and discover the Angkor Area by Tuk Tuk. Visit a elephant valley project in Mondulkiri.

Nature & Wildlife

Discover the impressive temples in Siem Reap, enjoy a close encounter with the rare and unique Irrawaddy dolphins near Kratie.

Pearls of Cambodia

Travel with the Bamboo Train in Battambang and enjoy the boat trip to the floating Villages on Tonle Sap. Relaxing stay in an Eco Lodge at the Tatai.
